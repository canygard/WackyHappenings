Confused Apple Music user posts other people's Spotify Wrapped screenshots on story

What does he listen to? What does he listen to? What does he listen to?

Don TendreDecember 1, 2021
Share this

Here at Wacky Happenings, December is a special time for us. This, of course, is due to the arrival of our favorite holiday: Spotify Wrapped Day. It’s a time where everyone takes to Instagram and Twitter to flaunt their unique and highly interesting taste in music.

This year’s Wrapped Day was no different from the rest, with Taylor Swifts, Drakes, and Kanyes visible as far as your thumb can tap through stories. That’s why it came as a shock when a local man, Brennan Wormwood, ruined the perfect stream of Spotify Wrapped screenshots with an atrocity so vile it’s difficult to put into words.

“There’s no correlation between any of these stats,” commented Anthony Fantano, Internet’s busiest music nerd. “It’s not possible for someone to like both Korn and Mike Stud. Either this guy is trolling, or he should be sent to a mental hospital.”

While it's clear to some that Wormwood was trolling, others remain confused on his motives. Has Apple Music turned him into an outcast? Was he simply mistaken about the trend? Was he truly just trying to fit in? The music community may never know the answers to these questions.
