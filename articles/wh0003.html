Desperate local woman creates Clash of Clans village to contact ex-boyfriend who blocked her on all platforms

Shortly after she gained access to the clan, she was removed for donating level  1 goblins to a fellow clan member.     

Sebastian Clashin October 31, 2021
Share this

"I was suspicious as soon as she joined the clan," claimed Brandon, better known by his Clash of Clans alias, gObLinMuNcHeR69. "Her T-hall was completely rushed and she had no idea how to make a proper base layout." The 26 year old CoC veteran told reporters he let Sally into his clan, The Tax Evaders, because he needed more clan members to participate in a war. "I wasn't ready to set a trophy limit on my clan. Advertising on Reddit simply didn't give me the manpower I was looking for."

When Sally was accepted with her low level village, deceptively named ClashOfClansGuy82 to avoid suspicion, she didn't quite know how to play the role she stepped into. When a fellow clan member asked for a donation of hog riders, she made the foolish mistake of offering up her base level goblins. "I just wanted some closure for all these years I spent with him. That's the least he could do!" After breaking up with me over Discord, he wouldn't let me say anything to change his mind! I know I can fix him if he would just give me a chance! If it weren't for those fucking goblins, he would have no idea I was there," she reminisced.

After kicking Sally out of the clan, Brandon began to see all of the red flags she had left prior to her damning mistake. The woman had used the clan chat to post her original poems about heartbreak and forgiveness, going as far as to include specific references to her 7 year relationship. At the time, Brandon had mistaken them for Radiohead lyrics. "You're going to have to try harder than that to win my gamer heart back."
