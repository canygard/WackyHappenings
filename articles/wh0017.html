Local gamer deletes Steam account; decides to be "just a regular guy" from now on

No more gaming. This guy has had enough.

Axel StoneMarch 1, 2022
Share this

The entire Discord server is in complete and total shock after the unexpected turn of events that transpired last night. Just 12 hours ago, the whole squad was playing Elden Ring, racking up some sick boss fights when suddenly, abruptly, one of the players disconnected. His fellow gamers assumed that once again, his Wi-Fi had betrayed him, causing him to temporarily pause his gaming session. They even considered the possibility of what actually occurred.

The gamer in question? Ian Bronson, formerly known as Sl0ppyD3m0nT0p. Before last night, he had been operating under this gamertag for a significant amount of time. Now, his Discord username is simply “Regular Guy.” What a sad and unfortunate moniker to bestow upon yourself.

"Yeah, I just decided the other day that I don't really wanna be a gamer anymore. I deleted my Steam account and took the RGB cat ears off of my headphones.” When asked what he will do with his free time, unburdened by the endless grind of the game, he let out a sigh of relief. “I think I’ll go outside. Maybe I’ll talk to a woman. There are really endless possibilities now that I’m just a regular guy.”
