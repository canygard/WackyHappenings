Heartless: Meet the man who didn't tell his IG followers what he was thankful for on his story

Mark Jenkins is thankful for nothing. No one. Not even his own brother.

Kenny JenkinsNovember 25, 2021
Share this

There’s nothing like Thanksgiving. Every year, Americans gather their least favorite family members for a politically tense, gluttonous dinner. Perhaps the most celebrated Thanksgiving tradition, however, is the expression of gratitude showcased through one of the most beautiful forms of communication man has ever seen: the Instagram story.

The endless barrage of photos depicting the friendships, families, and pets of literally everyone you know is one of the most time-honored traditions in social media history. Hours can be spent tapping (and swiping) through these stories while avoiding the insufferable people at the table in front of you. That’s why it came as a surprise to the followers of local man Mark Jenkins when no such story appeared on his profile.

“Shame on you,” commented an anonymous sibling. “Does our brotherhood mean nothing to you?

As this day comes to an end with no story in sight, I can solemnly put your question to rest, anonymous brother. Mark Jenkins doesn’t have an ounce of gratitude for you in his cold, unloving heart. I hope you see this, Mark. I hope this article haunts you to your core. I hope you feel the guilt you deserve to feel.
