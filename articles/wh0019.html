Elden Ring marketing team announces they will dox anyone who claims game is "mid"

From Software will not take any slander from their playerbase.

Sebastian ClasherMarch 21, 2022
Share this

The latest hit game, Elden Ring, has gotten gamers hyped to be indoors and hunched over their computers. You'd be hard-pressed to find anyone who doesn't have a positive thing to say about the title. The reason for the game's positive reception? Their intense marketing strategy: doxxing anyone who claims the game is "mid".

By programming bots to scour Twitter for messages containing "Elden Ring" and "mid", the marketing team at From Software is able to find perpetrators almost instantly. Once found, their hired private investigators will find their name, address, and phone number, which is then tweeted in response.

"You see," explained From Software lead marketing coordinator, Maria Poncio, "It's not a threat, per say. I would never threaten our loyal customers. Rather, I view it as a negative consequence to incentivize people not to make a mistake."
