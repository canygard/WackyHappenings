"I just think it's going to be a good outlet for my wacky, absurd sense of humor," said Wacky Happenings founder Connor Nygard, saving the Wacky Happenings logo on his desktop next to the plethora of unfinished projects.

"I don't get it," commented an anonymous friend. "Hasn't he heard of The Onion, ClickHole, or The Hard Times? There's just not an audience for one more satire news page. He's taken it too far this time!"

Nygard is notorious among his friends and family for sponteneously starting random business endeavors. Bands, clothing companies, Minecraft parody channels; no industry is safe from Nygard's clutches.
